Health & Well-Being

Women's health is a complex and multifaceted issue that requires attention and care throughout a woman's life. By staying informed, taking care of their bodies and minds, and seeking medical help when needed, women can take control of their health and live their best lives.

Menstruation is a natural and normal bodily process that occur in most women every month. During a period, the lining of the uterus sheds and is expelled from the body through the vagina. This typically lasts between 3 to 7 days, although the length and flow of periods can vary from person to person.

Sex ed is important because it can help individuals make informed decisions about their sexual health and reduce their risk of unintended pregnancy, STIs, and sexual violence. It also promotes healthy relationships and helps to break down stigmas surrounding sexuality and gender.

Psychological distress in women is a common and serious issue that can have a significant impact on a woman's overall well-being. Psychological distress can take many forms, including anxiety, depression, post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), and eating disorders, among others.
